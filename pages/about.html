About Eric

Nice to meet you!

Childhood Techie:

My passion for technology started during my childhood where I was fortunate to play an Atari 2600 (fun w/Pong), Nintendo (fun w/Mario, Zelda, etc.), and use a Macintosh Plus (fun w/MacPaint, HyperCard, etc.).

Education Techie:

In high school (early days of WWW) I joined the Technology Club and made websites including my class website. At Western Washington University, I majored in Management Information Systems (business and computers) and minored in Computer Science (Website Management & Design) and Mathematics. While there I founded a Mac User Group with my roommate and created multiple websites. Later I took education trainings, got a C# for Testers certificate at Bellevue College, and UX Design certificate at University of Washington.

Work Techie:

After college I was a Database Support Analyst for a real estate company and a top-selling iPod/Mac Specialist at an Apple Store. I happened to get a contract job at Microsoft as a Software Tester I and have enjoyed software testing with different companies for about 20 years.
